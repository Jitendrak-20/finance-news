Source Registry

Trusted feeds first, API upgrades later.

Latest Jobs

Published Articles

Clean editorial output with source attribution and separate SEO fields.

Each article is structured around headline, summary, why-it-matters context, source links, image, tags, and metadata.

Draft Review

Human review remains part of v1.

The blueprint recommends manual review during the first 30 to 60 days to control quality and hallucination risk.

Content Guardrails

Original structure, neutral tone, no advice language.

Use source-backed summaries rather than near-duplicate rewrites.

Reject unsupported claims, prices, dates, quotes, and exaggerated tone.

Generate separate meta title and description for each published article.

Keep category pages clean: share market, IPO, financial news, and global markets.
